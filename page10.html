In the second year, Emma followed Illustration in Luca School of Arts, with Gerda Dendooven and Isabelle Vandenabeele. This year contained lots of assignments wherein stories had to become images, and images needed to tell a story. It was not clear until the end of that year that Emma wasn't in the right place in Illustration. In the last few weeks before the jury in june 2017, Gerda told her to let go of the assignments and to make whatever she felt like making.  This is how the small series 'Fear' grew.
