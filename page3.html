2015-2016

In september 2015, Emma Boeyaert started her education at Luca School of Arts, following Drawing & Graphics/Illustration under supervision of Isabelle Vandenabeele, Steve Michiels and Peter Morrens. Assignments were given as open to both directions as possible, with the perpose to choose what the students wanted to follow after that first year. The content of drawings was finally free to choose, and the focus was no longer on techniques. All attention went to strong images now.
