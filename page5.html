Pictures as inspiration

2017-2018

In the third year at the school, now in Drawing & Graphics, Emma Boeyaert had a difficult start. For four months, she barely drew. Even though Illustration was not her ideal field of study, she was used to work on assignments. Drawing & Graphics is a different sort of education. Professors don't  come up with any assignment, so Emma had to make up for herself what she wanted to do. The intermediate jury in february was approaching, so in a shock of panic, Emma started painting with oilpaint quickly. Suddenly normal images surprised her. Taking pictures of everything that awakened a certain interest, she grew a small archive. This became the first database from where she built images to paint to. It was important to play, and to be amused while working. Also this stage slowly has to pass and at this moment, the perpose of an image asks for attention as well. The sketches Emma makes are mostly just tests for paintings, and in the paintings she tries to lay a subtile awkwardness or mystery.
